    ♦ HOW TO NAVIGATE ♦

    KEYBOARD

    • ← / → — previous / next filing group
    • SPACE — pause / resume the animation
    • ESC — close dialogs
    • ? — open / close this help

    CLICK TO EXPLORE

    • Politician name (on the auction card or in the feed) — open their holdings modal.
    • VIEW LATEST TRADES in the modal — filter the whole feed to that politician.
    • Row in the holdings table — deep-link to all of their trades of that asset.
    • Filing row in the sidebar feed — deep-link to that specific transaction.
    • VIEW PTR PDF (House) — open the House Clerk’s original disclosure PDF in a new tab.
    • VIEW SENATE FILING — opens eFD. First-time visitors must accept the Senate terms page, then click the link again; after that the session persists.

    URL PATTERNS

    • / — latest across all politicians
    • #/politician/<slug> — one politician
    • #/politician/<slug>/<yyyy_mm_dd> — one date
    • #/politician/<slug>/<yyyy_mm_dd>/<asset> — one trade
    • #/politician/<slug>/<asset> — one asset across dates

    SCENE LEGEND

    • ▲ green = purchase · ▼ red = sale
    • ⇄ round-trip = bought AND sold in the period (net may be $0)
    • SENATE banner appears for Senate trades (no state district on Senate filings)
    • Epilogue cards at the end of each scene print the filer’s verbatim description
